about me

i grew up outside of philadelphia and i go to school at the university of miami. i study computer science with minors in interactive media, film, and journalism. as of may 2021, i'm about to graduate college and recently got vaccinated. i'm just trying to take the end of this semester as slow as i can.

education

I studied at the university of miami for my undergrad education. I graduate May 14th, 2021 with my ba in computer science with minors in motion pictures, interative media, and journalism. in 2017 i graduated from highschool and moved to miami alone and began as a marine science computer science double major. after four years and a good bit of trial and error, i found the right fit for me. I chose to drop my marine major and instead got my scuba certification. With the new found space in my schedule i pursued my passion for media and content creation through my minors. through my film minor i found my workstudy job, joined delta kappa alpha, and university of miami television which have all given so much to my college experience. there's always more to learn but i feel i've gained a well rounded understanding of computer science and digital media that will serve me well.

abroad

in spring of 2019 i felt stagnant and chose to take another jump alone and went to rome to study abroad. i spent the semester taking screenwriting, directing, international journalism, 3d animation, and italian cinema. my semester abroad taught me so much about how many different ways there are for me to express myself creatively and that many of them easily work in conjunction with my coding background. once that clicked it opened up a whole new realm of creative possibilites and resolved a lot of the stuck feeling that was hanging over me for so long.
